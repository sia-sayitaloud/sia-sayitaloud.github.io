The Programme

Three phases. One transformation.

From first impressions to final negotiations — a structured journey through pre-placement, placement, and post-placement readiness.

Phase 01

Pre-Placement

Building the foundation — voice, stage presence, and the soft skills that precede every great first impression.

  • Voice modulation & stage presence
  • Communication fundamentals
  • Confidence & body language
Phase 02

Placement

The Career Activation Programme — interview mastery, diagnostic mock sessions, and corporate readiness that converts.

  • Live diagnostic mock interviews
  • Corporate HR expectations
  • Group discussion & panel readiness
Phase 03

Post-Placement

Sustaining momentum — workplace communication, AI literacy, and management skills for the first year and beyond.

  • Workplace communication
  • AI & management literacy
  • Career growth strategy
